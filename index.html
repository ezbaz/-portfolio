About Ezra's
practice.

Ezra Bazilevsky is based in Washington, DC and works under the handle ezbaz. The work centers on brand identity, packaging, logo systems, and illustration-led visual language with an emphasis on restraint, clarity, and character.

The homepage highlights a compact edit of selected projects and a process rooted in thoughtful systems rather than trends.
