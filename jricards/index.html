JRI Cards

A visual system for a growing brand.

JRI Cards needed a brand structure that could scale across products without losing clarity. The goal was to keep the identity simple enough for everyday use and strong enough for new directions.

The work focused on rhythm, hierarchy, and repeatable rules. Typography and spacing were treated as system tools first, then adjusted for expression where it mattered most.

The result is a visual language that stays consistent across decks, packaging concepts, and digital touchpoints while still leaving room for campaign-specific character.
